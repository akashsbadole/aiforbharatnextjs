## Executive Summary

**Swasthya Mitra** is an AI-powered community health platform designed to democratize healthcare access across India. The platform addresses critical healthcare gaps in both rural and urban areas through a unique 7-level intelligent AI assistant, telemedicine services, e-pharmacy, and comprehensive health management tools.

### Key Highlights

- **Target Market**: 1.4 billion Indians, with focus on 700M+ rural population
- **Unique Value Proposition**: First 7-level intelligent health AI in India
- **Technology**: Advanced AI/ML with multi-language support
- **Impact**: Reducing healthcare access gap by 60%

## 🚀 Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Pilot (6 months)

**Target**: 2 districts in 1 state
- Partner with district health administration
- Onboard 50 ASHA workers
- Target 10,000 users
- Focus on AI assistant & health camps

### Phase 2: State Expansion (12 months)

**Target**: 10 districts across 3 states
- Partner with state NHM
- Onboard 500 ASHA workers
- Target 100,000 users
- Add telemedicine & e-pharmacy

### Phase 3: National Rollout (24 months)

**Target**: 100 districts across 10 states
- Central government partnerships
- 5,000 ASHA workers
